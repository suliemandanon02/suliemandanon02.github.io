About Ahlul Quran Academy


Established in 1998

Ahlul Quran stands as a beacon of knowledge and faith, a non-profit Islamic organization founded in 1998 under the visionary leadership of Sr. Dalia and her devoted helpers. Dedicated to nurturing the hearts and minds of young boys and girls, Ahlul Quran tirelessly imparts the timeless wisdom of the Quran, Islamic studies, and the art of Tajweed. Through its unwavering commitment to education and community service, Ahlul Quran instills not only a deep understanding of the sacred texts but also fosters a profound connection to Islamic principles and values. With each lesson, it cultivates a generation empowered by knowledge, compassion, and a steadfast devotion to their faith, enriching lives and shaping futures with the light of Islam.

Embracing the universal principles of inclusivity and enlightenment, this organization's sole mission is to spread the illuminating teachings of Islam to all seekers of knowledge, regardless of background or creed. With open arms, it welcomes individuals from all walks of life, fostering an environment of understanding, respect, and dialogue. Rooted in the belief that knowledge transcends barriers, this organization tirelessly endeavors to share the rich tapestry of Islamic wisdom with sincerity and compassion. Through educational initiatives, outreach programs, and community engagement, it strives to dispel misconceptions and promote a deeper understanding of Islam's profound message of peace, justice, and unity. With each interaction, it seeks to inspire hearts and minds, nurturing a world where the light of knowledge shines brightly, guiding humanity towards harmony and enlightenment.
